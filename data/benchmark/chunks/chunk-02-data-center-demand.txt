Compute & Networking revenue. The year-over-year increase in the first quarter of fiscal year 2027 was due to growth in Data Center products, driven by the ramp of our Blackwell systems and demand for our InfiniBand, Spectrum-X Ethernet, and NVLink solutions. Graphics revenue. The year-over-year increase in the first quarter of fiscal year 2027 was driven by sales of our Blackwell architecture. Reportable segment operating income. The year-over-year increase in Compute & Networking segment operating income in the first quarter of fiscal year 2027 was driven by the growth in revenue and the non-recurrence of a $4.5 billion charge associated with H20 excess inventory and purchase obligations in the first quarter of fiscal year 2026. The year-over-year increase in Graphics segment operating income in the first quarter of fiscal year 2027 was driven by the growth in revenue.
