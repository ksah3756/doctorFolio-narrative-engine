Reportable Segments. Revenue by reportable segments for the three months ended April 26, 2026 and April 27, 2025 showed Compute & Networking revenue of $74.550 billion compared with $39.589 billion, a year-over-year increase of $34.961 billion, or 88%. Graphics revenue was $7.065 billion compared with $4.473 billion, a year-over-year increase of $2.592 billion, or 58%. Total revenue was $81.615 billion compared with $44.062 billion, an increase of $37.553 billion, or 85%. Compute & Networking operating income was $53.335 billion compared with $22.054 billion, up 142%. Graphics operating income was $2.941 billion compared with $1.640 billion, up 79%. Total reportable segment operating income was $56.276 billion compared with $23.694 billion, up 138%.
